1. Purpose
This policy outlines the standard operating procedures (SOPs) for
train operations, station management, safety compliance, and
maintenance coordination across all Kochi Metro stations.

2. Train Operations
- Minimum headway during peak hours: 6 minutes.
- All trains must undergo pre-departure brake tests.
- OCC approval required for unscheduled halts or route deviations.

3. Station Management
- Station controllers must submit hourly footfall updates.
- Public announcements must follow the approved messaging template.
- Emergency exits must remain accessible at all times.

4. Safety Protocols
- Daily safety inspection at every station before first service.
- Fire extinguishers must be checked once per week.
- Any safety incident must be logged in the OCC dashboard within 10 minutes.

5. Maintenance Coordination
- Track inspection every 48 hours.
- Rolling stock checks are mandatory after every 300 km of operation.
- All maintenance work orders must be digitally logged.

6. Compliance
Failure to follow the above guidelines may result in operational review,
penalties, or retraining as mandated by the Operations Manager.
